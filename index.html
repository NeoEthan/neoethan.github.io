Academic Hub 学术主页

My research examines the cognitive processes underlying individual innovation across different institutional or contextual settings. It specifically investigates the phenomenon of cognitive fixation and mechanisms for overcoming it. Methodologically, the work is predominantly empirical, relying on large-scale datasets. Additionally, my research also encompasses areas such as CEOs of publicly listed companies and financial analysts.

我主要关注在不同的创新制度下，个体创新的认知过程，研究主题围绕认知固化、以及如何打破认知固化，研究方法主要是基于大样本数据的实证研究。此外，我的研究还涉及上市公司CEO、金融分析师等领域。

Current Focus 当前研究重点

  • The relationship between the peer review system and innovation.
  • 同行评价制度与创新的关系
  • Recognition signals and post-success narrowing
  • 正式认定信号与成功后行为收敛
  • Failure experiences and subsequent evaluation behavior
  • 创作失败经历与后续评价行为
