我是杨光（Guang yang），华东政法大学商学院副教授、金融学硕士生导师。我主要关注在不同的创新制度下，个体创新的认知过程， 研究主题围绕认知固化及其破解；同时也关注上市公司 CEO、金融分析师等情境中的行为与绩效。

I am Guang Yang, Associate Professor at the Business School, East China University of Political Science and Law (ECUPL), and a Master's supervisor in Finance. My research examines cognitive processes of individual innovation under different innovation institutions, with a focus on cognitive fixation and how to break it. I also study listed-firm CEOs and financial analysts.
