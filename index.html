研究方向Research

我关注“创新制度如何塑造个体的认知与行为”，尤其是认知固化的形成与破解，以及评价制度对创新轨迹的影响。

I study how innovation institutions shape cognition and behavior, with particular attention to cognitive fixation, its mitigation, and how evaluation systems redirect innovation trajectories.

评价分歧与反思性学习 Evaluation Disagreement & Reflective Learning

当个体发现自己的评价与主流/权威裁决不一致时，如何触发反思性学习并打破认知固化，从而改变后续创新策略。

How disagreement between one's evaluation and majority/authority signals can trigger reflective learning, reduce fixation, and reshape subsequent innovation strategies.

正式认定信号与成功后收敛 Formal Recognition Signals & Post-success Narrowing

正式成功信息不仅奖励能力，也会改变认知注意与搜索空间，可能引发策略性收敛并抑制后续新颖性。

Formal success signals can reallocate attention and search, potentially causing strategic narrowing and lowering novelty later.

创作失败与后续评价行为 Creative Failure & Subsequent Evaluation Behavior

落选/失败如何通过自我认同威胁与换位理解等机制，影响个体对他人作品的评价严格性与宽容度。

How failure affects later evaluation of others via identity threat and perspective-taking, shaping strictness vs. leniency.

分歧之源，创新之泉：评价分歧如何影响同行评价参与者的创新？
Your Negation, My Opportunity: How Evaluation Disagreements Influence Innovation Among Peer-evaluation Participants
制度：同行评价Institution: peer evaluation 机制：认知失调→反思学习Mechanism: dissonance→reflection 状态：推进中Status: in progress

核心问题：当个体发现“我给低分，但平台/主流意见相反”时，如何触发反思性学习、打破认知固化并提升后续创新表现？ 研究强调认知灵活性与自我效能的边界条件，并以平台大样本行为数据进行检验。

Question: When a person realizes “I rated low but the platform/majority says otherwise,” does reflective learning reduce fixation and improve later innovation? Boundary conditions include cognitive flexibility and self-efficacy, tested using large-scale platform data.

大众创新之殇：成功（成功冲击）是否会阻碍创新？
The Dark Side of Success: Do Formal Recognition Shocks Narrow Subsequent Innovation?
制度：正式认定Institution: formal recognition 机制：认知固化Mechanism: cognitive fixation 状态：推进中Status: in progress

核心观点：区分“创作能力”（事前）与“成功冲击/正式认定信号”（事后），在控制能力后识别成功信息对后续创新的独立影响。 重点解释为何许多创作者“一次高峰后难以持续突破”。

Idea: Distinguish ex-ante creative ability from ex-post success shocks (formal recognition). After controlling for ability, identify the independent effect of success information on later innovation, helping explain why many creators peak once and then narrow.

昨日落榜，今日评卷：创作失败如何影响同行评价？
Yesterday’s Rejection, Today’s Grading: How Creative Failure Shapes Subsequent Peer Evaluation
理论：归因/自我肯定/换位Theory: attribution/affirmation/perspective 机制：双向效应Mechanism: two-sided effects 状态：推进中Status: in progress

核心观点：创作失败既可能威胁自我认同（更苛刻评价），也可能提升换位理解（更宽容评价）；两条路径随自我效能而变化。 结合平台数据与实验方法来识别心理机制。

Thesis: Failure can both threaten identity (harsher evaluation) and enhance perspective-taking (more lenient evaluation). The balance varies by self-efficacy, tested with platform data and experiments to identify psychological mechanisms.

公示成功 vs 正式采纳：不同成功信号为何对创新产生相反影响？
Public Recognition vs Official Selection: Why Different Success Signals Move Innovation in Opposite Directions
制度：社区 vs 权威Community vs authority 机制：激励 vs 固化Motivation vs fixation 状态：推进中Status: in progress

研究解释：公众认可可能增强动机与探索，而权威采纳更可能带来“终局性信号”与路径依赖，导致搜索空间收缩。 重点在于把两类成功信号的制度属性与认知后果区分开来。

Explanation: Public recognition can boost motivation and exploration, whereas official selection acts as a terminal signal that may increase path dependence and narrow search. The project distinguishes institutional properties and cognitive consequences of success signals.
